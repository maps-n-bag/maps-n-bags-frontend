August 14th — Exploring the Upper Engadine Valley

Badrutt's Palace Breakfast

Indulge in a classic continental spread while watching the mist rise from Lake St. Moritz. A historical landmark of elegance.

Muottas Muragl Hike

Ascend the funicular for a panoramic ridge walk. The view encompasses all four lakes of the Upper Engadine.

Navigating the Peaks

The route from Badrutt's to Muottas involves a short scenic train ride. Keep your camera ready for the crossing at the Punutresch Gorge.
